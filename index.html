The Story of Sakhi Pure

Deep within the ancient lanes of Varanasi, Saharsh Barnawal founded Sakhi Pure Masala with a simple vision: to bring the unadulterated taste of home-ground spices to every Indian

We believe that spice is not just an ingredient, but the soul of the meal. Our spices are sourced directly from farmers and ground using traditional techniques to preserve their essential oils and aroma.
